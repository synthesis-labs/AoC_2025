# Advent of Code 2025

# How to take part?
1. Signup for Advent of Code [here](https://adventofcode.com) (using your github account is best)
1. Join our Labs leaderboard [here](https://adventofcode.com/2025/leaderboard/private/view/2306561) (use join code - 2306561-242f2386)
1. Join the Labs' AoC discord [here](https://discord.gg/2j8VSD8G2N) or on Synthesis general Teams [here](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/message/19:585b6ca2e2db4a5891effc453318206d@thread.skype/1762414187525?tenantId=94cfdb85-3d23-4849-a066-5cdad965ccd8&groupId=4f1b96eb-0ad7-44d2-b9e1-d47c860dab44&parentMessageId=1762414187525&teamName=Synthesis&channelName=General&createdTime=1762414187525)
1. If you would like to share your solutions please feel free to create yourself a folder (maybe with your name, e.g. "tom") and push (or PR first and we will add you as a contributor thereafter)

Any issues please reach out to Mike, Tom, Tjaard, Bhavesh, Marais, or Francois. for help!

# The Awards

## Award 1: *The Thing of Beauty*

    the cleanest and most elegant solution that makes our jaws drop

## Award 2: *The Absolute Pits* 

    the most criminally insane solution that defies logic, but somehow works?

## Award 3: *Eye of the Beholder*

    the most idiomatic solution capturing the spirit of the language chosen

## Award 4: *The Rube Goldberg*

    that solution that simply had to show off

## Award 5: *The Ultimate Beast*

    the individual who simply crushed it
